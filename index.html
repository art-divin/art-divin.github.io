No more searching on the web for such a simple missing bits from the Xcode editor as working with JSON, Base64, HEX, ByteArray!
✔ Data Type Conversion

JSON, Base64, XML, HEX, ByteArray. These and many more to come
are available for creating data Conversion Strategies that you need!
Easy to create, Conversion Strategy can accept input either from text,
or from a file.
✔ Xcode Extension

Xcode support is built-in for most of the data types! Press "Save" button for the Conversion Strategy you want, and after relaunching Xcode it will appear in the Editor -> Data Converter menu item. Bind the item to a key combination and your completely custom Conversion Strategy will by right under your fingers!
✔ Drag & Drop Support

Drag & Drop support enhances the speed of data conversion. No need to think ahead about which data type you need. Just Drag & Drop file / text selection into the Conversion Strategy view. The result will appear immediately!
✔ Feedback is Appreciated!

Need more Data Types, or having any suggestions / ideas for improvements? Don't hesitate to contact at Twitter, or leave your feedback at the App Store and it will be appreciated!
